What is SlotAvailability?

SlotAvailability is building a global database that helps players check where slot providers are available by country and casino. The MVP currently tracks 10 major providers across 10 priority countries on Stake.

Availability data is manually reviewed and will be updated regularly. Future versions will include more casinos, more countries, provider statistics and an interactive world map.
